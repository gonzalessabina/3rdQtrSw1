Summary In 1919, following the Great War, Britain is a mixture of hopelessness and hedonism. In a country that is being shaken by economic turmoil, returning military, recently minted revolutions, and criminal gangs are battling for survival. The Peaky Blinders, led by returning war hero Thomas Shelby and his family, is one of the most potent gangs at the time. Thomas, though, has more aspirations than just to be a street hustler. He sees a chance to improve in life when a box of guns disappears because while crime may pay, legal business pays better. Inspector Chester Campbell, who comes from Belfast, is attempting to rid Britain of its crime.

CHARACTERS Tommy Shelby Arthur Shelby Polly Gray Alfie Solomons
